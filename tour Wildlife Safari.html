Wildlife Safari

The “Wildlife Safari” tour is an adventure into the heart of Indonesia’s wildlife. Explore the rainforests of Borneo and see orangutans in their natural habitat, go on a river safari to spot crocodiles and proboscis monkeys, and visit the Elephant Conservation Center in Sumatra.

2 - 8

3,000

6 days

Day 1: Jakarta

Arrive at Soekarno-Hatta International Airport in Jakarta
Visit the National Museum to learn about the history and culture
Explore the old town of Jakarta, including the Fatahillah Square
Visit the Jakarta History Museum

Day 2: Borneo

Early morning flight to Pangkalan Bun
Visit the Tanjung Puting National Park to see orangutans
A wildlife safari right in the heart of Borneo's jungle

Day 3: Borneo

Go on a river safari to spot crocodiles and proboscis monkeys
Visit the Camp Leakey research center to learn about orangutans
Visit Danum Valley Nature Preserve, an ancient rainforest

Day 4: Sumatera

Early morning flight to Sumatera
Visit the Elephant Conservation Center to learn about elephants
Visit to the Kerinci Seblat National Park to see rare Sumatran tigers
Сrossing the equator at Bonjol

Day 5: Bukit Lawang

Visit Bukit Lawang to see orangutans in their natural habitat
Trek through the rainforest to see other wildlife: gibbons, macaques
Visit Gunung Leuser National Park, home of elephants and tigers

Day 6: Lombok

Visit traditional Sasak village in Lombok
Transfer to Lombok International Airport for departure flight
