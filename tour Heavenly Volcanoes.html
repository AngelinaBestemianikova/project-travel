Heavenly Volcanoes

The “Heavenly Volcanoes” tour takes you on a bird's eye view tour of some of Indonesia's most spectacular volcanoes. Enjoy unusual views of the mountain peaks, which will be revealed to you in all their beauty.

2 - 5

1,500

5 days

Day 1: Bali

Arrive at Ngurah Rai International Airport in Bali
Visit to Mount Batur, an active volcano in Bali
Trekking to the summit for a stunning sunrise view

Day 2: Bandung

Depart for Bandung
Visit Tangkuban Perahu volcano
Visit Ciater hot springs

Day 3: Yogyakarta

Early morning transfer to Yogyakarta
Visit Mount Merbabu
Visit Borobudur Temple and Barong Temple
Climbing the Mount Nglanggeran

Day 4: Malang

Depart for Malang
Visit Mount Bromo and Mount Semeru Volcano
Rest on the Teluk Asmara beach
Take a boat to Sempu Island

Day 5: Surabaya

Depart for Surabaya
Visit Mount Ijen
Transfer to Ngurah Rai International Airport for departure
