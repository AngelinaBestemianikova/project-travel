Cultural Immersion

The “Cultural Immersion” tour is a unique opportunity to experience the rich culture and traditions of Indonesia. Visit the ancient temples of Borobudur and Prambanan, learn about traditional batik making in Yogyakarta, and witness a traditional dance performance in Bali.

5 days

Day 1: Yogyakarta

Arrive at Adisucipto International Airport
Visit the Sultan's Palace (Kraton) and Taman Sari Water Castle
Learn about traditional batik making at a local workshop

Day 2: Borobudur

Visit Borobudur Temple, a UNESCO World Heritage Site
Explore the temple and learn about its history and significance
Visit nearby temples such as Mendut and Pawon

Day 3: Prambanan

Visit Prambanan Temple, another UNESCO World Heritage Site
Watch the Ramayana Ballet performance at Prambanan Temple
Explore the temple and learn about its history and significance
Visit nearby temples such as Plaosan and Sewu

Day 4: Bali

Fly to Bali
Visit the Uluwatu Temple and watch the traditional Kecak Dance
Visit the Garuda Wisnu Kencana Cultural Park
Visit the Garuda Wisnu Kencana Cultural Park

Day 5: Ubud

A walk through the Tegalalang Rice Terrace
Visit the Ubud Monkey Forest
Visit the Ubud Palace and Ubud Market
