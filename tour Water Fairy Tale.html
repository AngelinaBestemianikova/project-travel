Water Fairy Tale

The “Heavenly Volcanoes” tour takes you on a bird's eye view tour of some of Indonesia's most spectacular volcanoes. Enjoy unusual views of the mountain peaks, which will be revealed to you in all their beauty.

1,000

4 days

Day 1: Jakarta

Arrive at Soekarno-Hatta International Airport in Jakarta
Visit the National Museum of Indonesia to learn about history, culture
Enjoy a traditional Indonesian dinner

Day 2: Pangandaran

Depart from Jakarta to Pangandaran, a coastal town in West Java
Visit the Green Canyon, a beautiful river with crystal clear water
Take a boat ride along the river and enjoy the scenery
Visit the Batu Karas Beach, a popular surfing spot
Enjoy a seafood dinner at a local restaurant

Day 3: Bogor

Transfer to the hotel in Bogor
Visit the Bogor Botanical Gardens, which is home to a variety of plant species, including rare orchids and palms
Rafting the Chilliwung River in traditional boats
Enjoy a traditional Indonesian dinner at a local restaurant

Day 4: Sumatera

Depart for Lake Toba, the largest volcanic lake in the world
Take a boat ride to Samosir Island, located in the middle of Lake Toba Visit the village of Ambarita, which is home to ancient stone chairs
Transfer to Sultan Syarif Kasim Airport for departure flight
