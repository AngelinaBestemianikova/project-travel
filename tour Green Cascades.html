Green Cascades

The “Green Cascades” tour is a unique journey into the green tropical forests and beautiful waterfalls of Indonesia. Feel the magic of nature and enjoy the crystal clear water that falls from the mountain slopes, creating cascades of amazing beauty.

2 - 12

1,200

5 days

Day 1: Jakarta

Arrive at Soekarno-Hatta International Airport in Jakarta
Visiting Ragunan Zoo to see orangutans and varans
Visiting Taman Suropati Park

Day 2: Bogor

Depart for Bogor, a city known for its botanical gardens
Visit the Bogor Botanical Gardens and explore the lush greenery
Continue the journey to Puncak, a mountain resort area

Day 3: Bandung

Visit the Taman Safari Indonesia, a wildlife park
Depart to Bandung, a city known for its climate and beautiful scener
Continue the journey to Ciater, a hot spring resort area
Free time to explore the city on your own

Day 4: Bandung

Visit Tangkuban Perahu, an active volcano with a beautiful crater
Malabar Tea Plantation Excursion
Walking in the Puncak Bintang Conservation Area
Tour of the Forest Park Conservation Area

Day 5: Jakarta

Depart for Jakarta
Visit the Taman Mini Indonesia Indah, a cultural park
Transfer to the airport for departure flight
