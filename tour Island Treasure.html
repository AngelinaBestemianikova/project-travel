Island Treasure

The "Island Treasure" tour is a trip to some of the most beautiful islands in Indonesia. Visit the famous Gili Islands, Lombok, and Komodo Island and enjoy snorkeling in crystal clear waters. Each island is a special place, which is not like the others.

2 - 8

2,000

7 days

Day 1: Bali

Arrive at Ngurah Rai International Airport in Bali
Transfer to hotel in Kuta or Seminyak area
Free time to explore the area and relax

Day 2: Gili Islands

Early morning transfer to Padang Bai harbor
Take a speedboat to Gili Trawangan
Enjoy the sunset on the beach
Overnight stay on Gili Trawangan

Day 3: Gili Islands

Explore Gili Meno and Gili Air
Snorkeling and swimming in crystal clear waters
Overnight stay on Gili Trawangan

Day 4: Lombok

Take a boat to Lombok Island
Visit traditional Sasak village
Explore the beautiful beaches of Lombok
Overnight stay in Lombok

Day 5: Komodo Island

Visit the traditional Sasak village of Sade
Trek to Sendang Gile and Tiu Kelep waterfalls
Take a flight to Labuan Bajo
Trekking to see the Komodo dragons

Day 6: Komodo Island

Visit Rinca Island and Komodo National Park
Snorkeling in Manta Point
Transfer to Lombok International Airport
Fly to Labuan Bajo on Flores Island

Day 7: Departure

Return to Labuan Bajo
Transfer to the airport for departure flight
