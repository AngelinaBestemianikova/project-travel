Bogorsky Botanic Garden

In the province of West Java, on the outskirts of the Indonesian city of Bogor, there is a magnificent botanical garden, one of the oldest and largest in the world. It is one of the oldest and largest botanical gardens in the world, located at the foot of the extinct Salak Volcano. The Botanical Garden was founded by a group of scientists in 1817.

Plants of Bogorsky Botanical Garden are collected from different tropical countries of the world. There is a great variety of giant succulents, palms, bamboos, lianas, saprophytes and many other amazing plants. But a special pride of the botanical garden is a rich collection of orchids. There are about 550 species of these amazing flowers. Upon entering the park, visitors find themselves in a shady alley of century-old trees covered with lianas. The lianas are a real ornament of the garden and a marvelous sight.

The garden strikes a look with beauty and violence of paints, it is beautiful at all seasons of the year. Here continuously blossom and fructify a plant over which butterflies of the most different sizes turn, the singing of birds is audible. The garden ponds topped with lotuses are very beautiful.
