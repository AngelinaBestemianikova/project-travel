Volcano Adventure

The “Volcano Adventure” tour is an exciting journey to explore the volcanic landscapes of Indonesia. Climb the famous Mount Bromo, Kawah Ijen and Mount Rinjani, and witness the breathtaking views of the sunrise over the volcanic craters.

2 - 8

1,800

6 days

Day 1: Surabaya

Arrive at Juanda International Airport in Surabaya
Visit the House of Sampoerna, a museum and cigarette factory
Explore the historic Arab Quarter of Surabaya

Day 2: East Java

Drive to Mount Penanjakan to see the sunrise over Mount Bromo
Enjoy the stunning view of the sunrise and the volcanic landscape
Drive to the foot of Mount Bromo and hike to the crater
Visit the Savana and Teletubbies Hill

Day 3: Paltuding

Drive to Paltuding and start the trek to Kawah Ijen
Witness the blue flames and sulfur mining activities
Enjoy the stunning view of the turquoise crater lake
Drive to Senggigi in Lombok

Day 4: Senaru

Drive to Senaru and start the trek to Mount Rinjani
Enjoy the stunning view of the volcanic landscape and the sunrise
Trek to the crater rim and camp overnight

Day 5: Lombok

Trek to the summit of Mount Rinjani (3,726 meters)
Enjoy the stunning view of the sunrise
Camp overnight

Day 6: Lombok

Trek back to the crater rim and descend to Senaru
Transfer to Lombok International Airport for your departure flight
