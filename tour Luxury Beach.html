Luxury Beach

The “Luxury Beach” tour is a relaxing getaway to some of Indonesia’s most beautiful beaches. Stay in luxury villas in Bali and Lombok, enjoy private beach access, and indulge in spa treatments and gourmet meals.

2 - 4

3,200

6 days

Day 1: Bali

Arrive at Ngurah Rai International Airport in Bali
Meet and greet with tour guide
Transfer to luxury villa in Seminyak
Enjoy welcome drinks and dinner at the villa

Day 2: Seminyak

Spend the day at Seminyak Beach with private beach access
Activities include surfing, beach volleyball, and relaxing on the beach
Return to villa for spa treatments and gourmet dinner

Day 3: Bali

Visit Tanah Lot Temple, a famous Hindu temple in the sea
Visit Ubud, a cultural and artistic center of Bali
Return to villa for dinner

Day 4: Lombok

Transfer to Ngurah Rai International Airport for flight to Lombok
Arrive at Lombok Airport and transfer to luxury villa in Senggigi
Enjoy welcome drinks and dinner at the villa

Day 5: Senggigi

Spend the day at Senggigi Beach with private beach access
Activities include snorkeling, kayaking, or simply relaxing on the beach
Return to villa for spa treatments and gourmet dinner

Day 6: Bali

Visit Tanjung Benoa Beach for turtle conservation center, swimming
Transfer to Lombok International Airport for departure flight
